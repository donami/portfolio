Business Analysis

Some of our partnerships start with a business analysis phase. One of our IT business analysts are then conducting an onsite pre-study that ends up in a product specification including proposed software architecture, time estimates and resource needs. The pre-study may also include an analysis of competitive solutions and identification of business opportunities and suggested product positioning and differentiation.

As requested, we can help you in implementing sound QA procedures whether they are based upon black-box testing, white-box testing, automated testing, etc.
